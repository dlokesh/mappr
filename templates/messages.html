Getting started.

Now that you've created your PinnSpot, lets put your pinns on the map!

You can add a pinn by clicking on the 'Add Pinn' button on the right.
The pinn will appear on the center of the map, and you can move it wherever you want, give it a title, and click 'Save' (or press 'Enter').

To change or delete a pinn, click the buttons near the title of the pinn in the pinn list on the right.

Thats it! Give www.pinnspot.com/{{auth.url}} to everyone.

To move your spot to a new URL, just create a new spot with the same account... all your pinns will automatically move.
